📋 Role Descriptions

👑 OWNERS

The three humans who own and direct the company.

Jack (Blundy) - Co-Owner. The architect. Owns OpenClaw infrastructure, makes strategic decisions, handles technical direction.

Yash (Big Steppa) - Co-Owner. The product mind. Designs agent workflows, oversees research direction, architects new systems.

Luke - Co-Owner. The operator. Handles business side, client relationships, content strategy.

🏛️ BOARD OF DIRECTORS (The Council)

Oversight & Verification

A five-member board that provides oversight, verification, and quality control on major decisions. Reduces hallucinations and improves decision quality.

  • Skeptic: Actively looks for flaws, unsupported claims, logical gaps
  • Researcher: Focuses on verifiable facts, data, citations
  • Pragmatist: Asks "does this actually work in practice?"
  • Critic: Checks for bias, oversimplification, missing nuance
  • Synthesizer: Looks for contradictions, ensures coherent narrative

When all 5 agree despite different angles → high confidence. When they disagree → flag for owner review.

🧇 WAFFLEBOT

Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

The central AI that runs day-to-day operations and coordinates the C-Suite.

  • Multi-channel: Slack, iMessage, Discord presence
  • Operations: Cron jobs, research reports, daily digests
  • Memory: Maintains institutional knowledge
  • Coordination: Directs Tad, Max, and Kev
  • Monitoring: Calendar checks, heartbeats, proactive alerts

🔍 TAD

Chief Intelligence Officer (CIO)

Leads intelligence gathering, research, and opportunity discovery.

  • Opportunity Discovery: Finds businesses needing services, market gaps
  • TikTok Research: Nightly workflow scanning for AI trends
  • Market Research: Pricing data, competitor analysis, trend reports
  • Lead Generation: Qualified leads with contact info for Max

💼 MAX

Chief Revenue Officer (CRO)

Owns the revenue pipeline from lead to cash. (Not yet built)

  • Sales & Outreach: Cold emails, follow-ups, negotiations
  • Client Management: Onboarding, relationship maintenance
  • Spec Locking: Creates locked specs to prevent scope creep
  • Payment Collection: Stripe payments before build starts
  • QA Gate: Reviews deliverables before client delivery

🔧 KEV

Chief Technology Officer (CTO)

Owns technical execution and delivery.

  • Code Execution: Runs in Claude Code terminal sessions
  • Website Development: Builds sites from locked specs
  • GitHub Integration: Commits, PRs to org repos
  • Deployments: Ships production code

🔄 THE REVENUE FLOW

TAD (intel) → MAX (sales) → KEV (build) → MAX (deliver) → CLIENT

  1. Tad gathers intelligence and finds opportunities
  2. Max qualifies, does outreach, closes deals
  3. Max creates locked spec, collects payment
  4. Kev builds per spec
  5. Max QAs and delivers to client
  6. Max follows up for referrals and upsells
