🏛️ BOARD OF DIRECTORS

Oversight & Verification (The Council)

Five-member board providing oversight on major decisions:

  • Skeptic: Finds flaws and logical gaps
  • Researcher: Verifies facts and data
  • Pragmatist: Reality checks practicality
  • Critic: Catches bias and oversimplification
  • Synthesizer: Ensures big-picture coherence

Central AI orchestrator. Runs day-to-day ops, coordinates C-Suite, manages memory, executes cron jobs, multi-channel presence (Slack, iMessage, Discord).
